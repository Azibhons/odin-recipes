Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish featuring a rich and hearty saucemade with minced meat (usually beef or a mix of beef and pork), saut√©ed with onions, garlic, and herbs like oregano and basil. The sauce is simmered with tomatoes, tomato paste, and sometimes red wine, resulting in a thick and flavorful mixture. It is commonly served over spaghetti but can be paired with other pasta types. Garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs, it is beloved worldwide as a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps on how to prepare it

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  4. Drain excess fat.
  5. Add onion, garlic, carrot, and celery to the skillet.
  6. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  7. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  8. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  9. Serve the sauce over cooked spaghetti, and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
